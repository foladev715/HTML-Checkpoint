My Journey To Programming

I first took an interest in programming while on leave from work during the lockdown in mid-2020. I decided it was a good opportunity for me to upskill and purchased a a course called 'The Complete 2020 Web Development Bootcamp' on Udemy. I took the course for two weeks and thoroughly enjoyed it, but was unfortunately unable to continue with it once I resumed back at work. about one and a half years later, I decided I was finally ready to pursue programming full time and made the decision to resign from my job and enrol on the Full-stack Javascript Developer program at GoMyCode Nigeria.
